DLL Sideloading (Defense Evasion)

SNAPPYBEE delivered as a DLL alongside legitimate executables from the following antivirus products:
- Norton Antivirus
- Bkav Antivirus
- IObit Malware Fighter

Technique: DLL Search Order Hijacking / DLL Sideloading (T1574.001 / T1574.002)

Notable overlap with Trend Micro Earth Estries IOC list:
- WINMM.dll, DgApi.dll, imfsbDLL.dll, dbindex.dat, NortonLog.txt all appear as
  SNAPPYBEE loader/payload filenames in both Darktrace and Trend Micro reporting,
  confirming consistent tooling across campaigns.


Pivot Infrastructure

Initial access activity originated from an endpoint associated with SoftEther VPN service (T1665),
consistent with Salt Typhoon's known use of SoftEther for infrastructure obfuscation.
Trend Micro's Earth Estries IOC list also documents SoftEther VPN nodes in both Campaign Alpha and Beta.
